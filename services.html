Services

Inspection-to-Action Services

From drone flight operations to CMMS work order creation — every service is designed to move inspection findings into your maintenance workflow without manual data entry.

Core Service

Drone Roof & Exterior Inspections

Commercial drone inspections using DJI enterprise platforms with visual and thermal payloads. Every flight is conducted by FAA Part 107 certified pilots with full insurance coverage and airspace authorization.

Visual Assessment

High-resolution RGB imagery at sub-inch ground sample distance. Membrane condition, flashing integrity, ponding water, penetration seals, and vegetation growth documented and georeferenced.

Thermal Imaging

Infrared radiometric capture for moisture detection, insulation deficiency mapping, and HVAC performance evaluation. Temperature differentials quantified and classified by severity.

Photogrammetry & 3D Models

Orthomosaic generation, digital surface models, and 3D point clouds for measurement, area calculations, and volumetric analysis. Delivered in standard GIS formats.

Integration

CMMS & ERP Data Integration

The differentiator. We don't just deliver reports — we push structured data directly into your maintenance management system.

Work Order Generation

Inspection findings are mapped to your CMMS work order schema with the correct asset identifiers, location codes, priority levels, and trade classifications. Work orders are created automatically via API or secure data import.

Asset Record Updates

Condition scores, inspection dates, and supporting imagery are attached to the correct asset records in your system. Historical condition data builds over time for lifecycle analysis.

Supported Platforms

IBM Maximo, Microsoft Dynamics 365 (F&O, Business Central), FIIX, UpKeep, SAP PM/EAM, and other platforms with REST API or CSV import capabilities. Custom integrations available.

Data Mapping & Transformation

We handle the translation between inspection terminology and your system's data model. Asset hierarchies, classification codes, and custom fields are mapped before the first integration run.

Additional Services

Beyond the Flight

Condition Assessments

Standardized scoring aligned to ASTM, BOMA, or your internal framework. Severity classifications, remaining useful life estimates, and repair cost projections included.

Data & Reporting

Comprehensive deliverables including annotated imagery, orthomosaics, thermal maps, structured CSV/JSON exports, and executive summaries. All data georeferenced and timestamped.

Integration Consulting

Guidance on connecting drone and sensor data to your asset management ecosystem. Technology evaluation, API architecture, process design, and change management for maintenance teams.
