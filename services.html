Program Details

Program 01

Essential Asset Watch

A recurring visual inspection program for the assets every facility manager is already accountable for. Annual or semi-annual cycles, severity-rated findings, and a structured deliverable that your CMMS can ingest — without standing up a full reliability function.

Target Assets

Roofs, building envelope, facades, parapets, primary exterior structures. Single-site or small portfolio.

Cadence

Annual standard. Semi-annual for older roofs, post-storm follow-ups, or assets approaching end of useful life.

What You Get

Severity-rated defect log, georeferenced photo evidence, condition score, and a CMMS-ready findings file with asset tags, location codes, and recommended priorities.

Best Fit

Facility managers and property owners who want a defensible condition record on a predictable cycle without a custom integration build.

Program 02

Critical Asset Assurance

A quarterly visual and thermal inspection program for assets where unplanned failure is the dominant operational risk. Severity ratings, trend reporting across cycles, and a 24-hour notification path on critical findings — built so your maintenance team is the first to know, not the last.

Target Assets

Crane runways, overhead structures, rooftop mechanical infrastructure, solar arrays, tank exteriors, elevated systems, stormwater assets, hard-to-reach process infrastructure.

Cadence

Quarterly visual + thermal as standard. Trigger-event response (post-storm, post-incident, pre-shutdown) included in scope.

What You Get

Visual + thermal findings, severity ratings, trend reporting across cycles, 24-hour critical finding notification, CMMS-ready work order records every cycle.

Best Fit

Manufacturing, distribution, utilities, energy, and other operations where unplanned downtime carries direct production or safety cost.

Program 03

Reliability Program

A managed inspection program with CMMS integration. Stratum scopes the asset register, sets cadence per asset class, ingests findings directly into your maintenance system, and reviews trends and program performance with you every cycle. This is the program for organizations replacing ad-hoc inspection spend with a planned reliability function.

Target Assets

Multiple asset classes across one or more sites — roofs, overhead structures, crane runways, elevated systems, solar arrays, tanks, facades, rooftop mechanical infrastructure, stormwater assets.

Cadence

Set per asset class based on risk, age, and operating context. Reviewed and adjusted at the cycle program review.

What You Get

Direct CMMS integration (IBM Maximo, Microsoft Dynamics 365, FIIX, UpKeep, SAP, or comparable), trended condition history, scheduled program review meetings, and a single accountable point of contact for inspection operations.

Best Fit

Facility leaders standardizing inspection practice across a portfolio, or replacing one-off vendor spend with a planned, trendable, defensible program.

Pricing Approach

Annual, Recurring, Scope-Based Programs — Not Commodity Flights

Stratum doesn't quote a per-flight rate. Each program is priced on the scope of work that actually drives maintenance value over a year of operation.

Enrolled Assets

Which assets are in the program. Roofs, overhead structures, crane runways, mechanical, solar, tanks, facades, stormwater assets — scoped to your asset register.

Square Footage / Asset Count

Roof area, number of crane runs, number of solar panels or tank exteriors, number of buildings or sites. The volume of inspection work over a cycle.

Frequency

Quarterly, semi-annual, or annual cadence per asset class. Higher cadence on critical assets, lower cadence on stable assets — the program reflects the risk, not a flat rate.

Trigger-Event Response

Post-storm, post-incident, or pre-shutdown inspections included in scope so you don't negotiate a new SOW every time something happens.

CMMS Integration Depth

Structured findings file, structured work order records, or direct CMMS API feed. Pricing reflects the integration build and the ongoing operation of that pipeline.

Program Review

Recurring program review meetings with your facility, reliability, or maintenance leadership — included in Critical Asset Assurance and Reliability Program scopes.
