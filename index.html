Inspection Programs Built for Maintenance Teams

Our Programs

Three Recurring Programs. One Maintenance-Ready Output.

Choose the cadence and depth that matches the risk profile of your assets — from annual roof watch to managed reliability programs with CMMS integration.

Essential Asset Watch

Annual or semi-annual visual inspection of roofs and primary exterior assets. Severity-rated defect log, georeferenced photo evidence, and a structured findings deliverable (PDF and CSV) after each flight. The baseline program for facility teams that want a defensible condition record without standing up a full reliability function.

Best for: Roofs, facades, building envelope — single-site or small portfolio.

Cadence: Annual or semi-annual. Output: PDF + CSV findings.

Learn More

Critical Asset Assurance

Quarterly visual and thermal program on the assets where failure is most expensive — crane runways, overhead structures, rooftop mechanical, solar arrays, and tank exteriors. Severity ratings, trend reporting, an S1 alert within 4 hours with a written action summary within 24 hours, and CMMS-ready work order records after each flight.

Best for: Manufacturing, distribution, utilities, energy — anywhere unplanned downtime is the dominant risk.

Cadence: Quarterly visual + thermal. Output: Structured findings + CMMS-ready import.

Learn More

Reliability Program

Managed inspection program with direct CMMS integration. Stratum scopes the asset register, sets cadence per asset class, ingests findings directly into your maintenance system via API or managed feed, and reviews trends and program performance with you each cycle. Replaces ad-hoc inspection spend with a planned reliability function.

Best for: Facility leaders standardizing inspection practice across multiple assets, sites, or asset classes.

Cadence: Per asset class, managed. Output: Direct API or managed CMMS feed.

Our Process

How a Stratum Program Runs

Four steps from program design to maintenance-ready work orders — repeated every cycle.

01

Scope the Program

We walk your asset register with you, agree on which assets are in-scope, and set cadence by risk profile. Asset hierarchy, location codes, and severity framework are mapped to your CMMS before the first flight.

02

Inspect on Cadence

Recurring visual and thermal inspections on the schedule we agreed — quarterly, semi-annual, or annual. FAA Part 107 certified, fully insured, LAANC airspace authorization handled, scheduled around your operations.

03

Structure Findings

Every defect is georeferenced, severity-rated, and tagged to the right asset record. Findings are formatted as CMMS-ready records with the fields, codes, and priority levels your maintenance system expects.

04

Feed the Maintenance Workflow

Structured findings flow into your maintenance system — as a CSV/PDF for Essential Asset Watch, a CMMS-ready import for Critical Asset Assurance, or a direct API feed in the Reliability Program. S1 critical findings trigger an alert within 4 hours with a written action summary within 24 hours. Trend data builds across cycles into a defensible condition history.
