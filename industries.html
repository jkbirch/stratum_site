Industries

Built for Facility-Intensive Operations

We serve organizations that manage large physical asset portfolios and need inspection data integrated with their maintenance management systems.

Commercial Real Estate

Multi-tenant strip malls, office parks, and retail centers with diverse roofing portfolios. Stratum inspects across your entire portfolio and delivers findings organized by property, building, and asset — ready for your property management CMMS.

  • Multi-building portfolio inspections
  • Tenant-specific defect tracking
  • Capital planning data for roof replacements
  • Insurance documentation support

Industrial & Manufacturing

Plants, factories, and production facilities with large flat-roof footprints and complex HVAC systems. We understand the operational constraints of active manufacturing environments and schedule flights to minimize disruption.

  • Large flat-roof membrane assessments
  • HVAC and mechanical equipment surveys
  • Thermal performance mapping
  • Integration with Maximo, SAP, D365

Institutional

Churches, schools, hospitals, and government buildings with complex architectural roofing systems — steep slopes, multiple levels, valleys, and penetrations that are difficult and dangerous to inspect manually.

  • Complex roof geometry mapping
  • Steeple and facade inspections
  • ADA and safety compliance documentation
  • Grant and insurance claim support

Utilities & Energy

Power generation stations, substations, transmission corridors, and solar installations. Drone inspections reduce outage risk by identifying equipment degradation before failure in environments where manual access is restricted or hazardous.

  • Substation equipment thermography
  • Solar panel defect detection
  • Cooling tower and stack inspections
  • Regulatory compliance documentation

Warehousing & Logistics

Distribution centers and fulfillment warehouses with massive roof areas where leaks directly threaten inventory. Fast, non-disruptive inspections that keep operations running while we capture comprehensive condition data.

  • 100,000+ sq ft roof surveys
  • Moisture intrusion detection
  • Loading dock and facade assessment
  • Integration with WMS-adjacent CMMS

Insurance & Risk Management

Supporting insurance carriers, adjusters, and risk engineers with objective, data-driven assessment documentation. Consistent methodology across properties enables portfolio-wide risk comparison.

  • Pre-loss condition documentation
  • Claims support imagery
  • Portfolio risk scoring
  • Standardized reporting formats
